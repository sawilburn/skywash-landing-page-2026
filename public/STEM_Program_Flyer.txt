PROGRAM OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Join us for a 3-week intensive apprenticeship beginning in late May 2026.

Learn how heavy-lift drone technology is revolutionizing the exterior cleaning
industry while gaining hands-on experience in one of the most complex and
regulated airspaces in the United States.

Duration: 3 Weeks
Location: Purcellville, VA
Start Date: Late May 2026

================================================================================
THREE CORE PILLARS
================================================================================

1. COMPLEX AIRSPACE OPERATIONS

   Learn how to operate UAS safely within the DC SFRA (Special Flight Rules
   Area) and FRZ (Flight Restricted Zone), and understand the certification
   process:

   • FAA regulations and compliance requirements
   • Flight planning in restricted zones
   • Understanding the path to certification

2. CHEMICAL SCIENCE

   Deep dive into chemical safety and the eco-friendly solutions used in
   aerial soft washing:

   • Environmental chemistry fundamentals
   • Biodegradable cleaning agents
   • Safety data sheets and handling protocols

3. THE FUTURE OF SERVICE

   Real-world application of UAS technology in the modern workforce:

   • Industry trends and emerging technologies
   • Business operations and client management
   • Hands-on field experience
